Quarantining infected people is undoubtedly an excellent strategy to reduce the spread of the epidemic.
From now on, soon after a person is infected, they will be moved to a quarantine area.
Notice how if the contagiousness is high, even the brief period in which the infected person remains in the main area allows them to infect other people. A perfect quarantine however, like the one simulated, manages to effectively halt the expansion.
